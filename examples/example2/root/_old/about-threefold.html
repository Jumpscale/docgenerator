The need for capacity is constantly growing. In 10 years the world will use 15 times more internet capacity.

Today the capacity is generated by hyperscale data centers.

Data centers are built only in energy dense areas because they consume huge amounts of energy .

The problem with the internet today is that it is highly centralized.

Centralized internet is inefficient, expensive and wasteful.

Consider electricity:

If consumers in Turkey had to use electricity, generated in USA, it would be too expensive and inefficient, because it is too remote. The transmission lines would be too strained, losses and repairs would make it unsustainable. There would be multiple areas without access to electricity.

This is exactly what is happening to the internet today.
When internet is too remote to many users, it becomes expensive and wasteful. Many people have limited, if any access to internet.

Major technical problems with the internet are:

Latency
Bandwith
Autonomy
Privacy and Security

ThreeFold achieves it with its disruptive technology

Self healing cloud

A major efficiency gain comes from removing human requirement for deploying and operating IT infrastructure and services. To eradicate the human element in IT management, a new paradigm needed to be applied to architect, provision, deploy and operate IT infrastructures.

Best utilization of hardware

Efficient and scalable operating system that erases multiple layers and delivers compute and storage capacity everywhere, much closer to the hardware. Its thinness makes it cost efficient and energy saving.

And, ThreeFold internet is better for the planet, because it consumes a fraction of electricity that traditional data centers do.

This is achieved, among other things, through a standby mode that ThreeFold technology has. It is just like a sleep mode on your laptop, whereas traditional data centers are always “on” even if they are not being used.

ThreeFold Grid is live, launched in March 2018.
